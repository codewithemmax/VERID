# 🛡️ VERID

> **See Before You Pay.**

An AI-powered fraud detection platform that helps buyers identify suspicious marketplace listings **before making a payment.**

🏆 **Built for HackX Challenge 2026 | ECX 6.0 Powered by Union Bank**

---

##  Overview

Every day, thousands of online shoppers lose money to fraudulent marketplace listings because they have no reliable way to determine whether a seller is genuine before sending payment.

**VERID** changes that.

Powered by **Google Gemini AI**, VERID analyzes marketplace listings in real time by evaluating multiple fraud signals, including:

- 👤 Seller credibility
- 📝 Listing quality
- 💰 Price anomalies
- 🖼️ Product images
- 🚩 Suspicious behavioral patterns

Instead of simply displaying a generic warning, VERID explains **why** a listing appears risky in clear, human-friendly language—empowering buyers to make informed decisions before paying.

---

#  Features

### 🤖 AI Fraud Detection

Detects scam listings using multiple fraud signals simultaneously.

### 📊 Intelligent Risk Scoring

Generates a fraud score between **0–100** based on combined indicators rather than isolated rules.

###  Explainable AI

Every verdict includes an easy-to-understand explanation generated by **Google Gemini**.

###  Pre-Payment Protection

Protects buyers **before** payment—the moment when fraud prevention matters most.

###  Image Analysis

Detects stock images and AI-generated product photos using **Gemini Vision**.

###  Signal Stacking

Rather than relying on a single warning sign, VERID combines multiple weak signals to identify sophisticated scams.

###  Privacy First

- No buyer profiling
- No personal data stored
- Only publicly available listing information is analyzed

#  Risk Levels

| Score | Status | User Experience |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| 🟢 0–30 | Safe | Green badge with no interruption |
| 🟡 31–60 | Caution | Yellow warning banner |
| 🟠 61–85 | Suspicious | Orange warning with explanation |
| 🔴 86–100 | High Risk | Payment interruption modal |

#  Security & Privacy

VERID was designed with privacy as a core principle.

- Uses only publicly available marketplace data
- AI requests are processed server-side
- Fraud logs are anonymized
- No buyer tracking
- No personal profiles created

---

#  Why VERID?

Most online marketplaces detect fraud **after** victims have already lost money.

VERID shifts fraud prevention to the moment **before payment**, helping users avoid scams instead of attempting to recover stolen funds.

By combining AI-powered fraud detection with explainable insights, VERID gives buyers confidence to make safer purchasing decisions.
